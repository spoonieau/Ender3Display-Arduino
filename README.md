# Ender3Display-Arduino
Demo of a Ender3 Pro Display working on a Arduino Uno 

Dependencies 
U8g2lib https://github.com/olikraus/u8g2


Hookup, pin-out (EXP3) </br>
 ╔══____══╗</br>
 ║ 1 2 3 4 5 ..║</br>
 ║ 6 7 8 9 10 ║</br>
 ╚═══════╝</br>
 
 
 EXP3 Pins   |   Arduino Uno Pins
 --- | ---
 1   |   Ground
 2   |   D10 (CS)
 3   |   D3  (Enc B)
 4   |   D2  (Enc A)
 5   |   D9  (Buzzer)
 6   |   5+
 7   |   D11 (Data)
 8   |   D13 (Clk)
 9   |   D8  (Reset)
 10   |   D4  (PushBtn)
